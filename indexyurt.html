The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?

Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt canvas walls, a woodeb flood, and a roof dome that can be opened.

How are the yurts furnished?

Each yurt is furnished with a queensize bed wit down quilt and gas-fired stove. Your luxury camping experience include selectricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.

What should I bring?

Most guests pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with light layers of clothing.It's also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure!
